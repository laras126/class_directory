Fun Fact:

My middle name is Lee, after the confederate war general Robert E. Lee. When I was born, my parents thought we were related to him because a genealogist working on our family history had told them we were. (We lived in Nashville at the time, so this was big news.)

A few months after I was born, the genealogist called my parents to tell them that he had made a mistake. It wasn't Robert E. Lee we were related to, but in fact, some other general named Light Horse Harry.

Luckily, they decided to keep my middle name.
